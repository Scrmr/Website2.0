How to play ▸

Setup: Each player places exactly 10 cells on their side (Red = left half, Blue = right half), then presses Ready. Simulation begins once both are ready.

Simulation: The board evolves automatically for 10 generations. No input during this phase — just watch.

Reinforcement: After each simulation block, both players place 5–10 more cells and press Ready. The cycle repeats.

Survival rule: A cell lives if it has 2–3 same-colour neighbours and at most 3 total living neighbours. Otherwise it dies.

Birth rule: An empty cell with exactly 3 living neighbours is colonised by whichever colour has the majority among those neighbours.

Winning: Eliminate all opponent cells at any point, or have more cells when generation 250 is reached. Equal counts at the cap is a draw.
